1. Information Collection and Use

We collect information in the following ways:

  • iCal Calendar URL from Canvas LMS: When you provide your Canvas LMS calendar URL, we access your Canvas LMS calendar events.
  • Google Task Integration: We require access to your Google account to add Canvas LMS events as tasks in Google Tasks. We only access your Google Tasks data and no other Google services.

2. Data Processing

  • Event Synchronization: We process the events from your Canvas LMS calendar and create corresponding tasks in your Google Tasks list.
  • Data Storage: No personal data or event details are stored on our servers. All data processing occurs in real-time, and we do not maintain any databases of user information.

3. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your information.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers.

5. User Control and Access

  • Account Information: You may, at any time, review or change the information in your account or terminate your account by:
    • Logging into your Google account settings and updating the permission.
    • Contacting us using the contact information provided below.
  • Data Removal: If you wish to delete your integration with Google Tasks, you can contact kota113@keio.jp.
